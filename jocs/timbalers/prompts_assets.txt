# PROMPTS PER A "ELS TIMBALERS" (ESTIL CASINO / GAME SHOW)

Estil General: Glamour, Llums de Neó, Daurats, Cortines de Vellut Vermell. Estètica de "Concurs de Televisió Retro" o "Casino de Las Vegas".

## 1. ELEMENTS DEL JOC (PNG Transparent)

### TIMBAL "ORQUESTRA DEL SOR" (The Container)
**Prompt:** A luxurious orchestral timpani drum (kettle drum) made of polished copper and gold. Instead of a normal skin, the top has a glowing question mark neon sign on it. Shiny, expensive look. Isolated on transparent background.

### TIMBAL "PREMI" (Obert/Correcte)
**Prompt:** The same luxury timpani drum but open or with the top bursting with golden musical notes and confetti. Green neon glow indicating "Correct". Isolated on transparent background.

### TIMBAL "ERROR" (Fallada)
**Prompt:** The same luxury timpani drum but looking deflated or with a "Wrong" red X neon sign buzzing over it. Smoke coming out. Isolated on transparent background.

---

## 2. FONS (BACKGROUND) - JPG 16:9

### ESCENARI PRINCIPAL (Main Stage)
**Prompt:** A spectacular game show stage background. Red velvet curtains on the sides. In the center, a spotlight illuminatng three empty spots for the drums. Background has bokeh lights, sparkles, and a golden art-deco pattern. 1950s Casino glamour. Warm lighting.

### FONS DE PREGUNTA (Quiz Overlay)
**Prompt:** A semi-transparent overlay graphic. A stylish gold art-deco frame board where a question text matches the casino theme. Sleek and elegant.

## 3. UI EXTRAS

### BOTÓ "JUGAR" (Casino Chip)
**Prompt:** A large poker chip or casino token made of gold and red enamel. Text "PLAY" or "JUGAR" embossed in gold in the center. Shiny 3D render.

### TEXTURA DE NEÓ
**Prompt:** Neon tubes alphabet (A-Z) and numbers (0-9) in classic Vegas gold and signage style. (Optional, for creating custom titles).

## 4. MÚSICA I SO (AUDIO)

### BANDA SONORA (Quiz Show Jazz)
**Prompt:** Upbeat Big Band Jazz. Brass section (trumpets, trombones) hits, walking bass, swinging drums. Exciting "Game Show" anticipation music. Think "The Price is Right" meets "Casino Royale".

### EFECTE: REDOBLE (Drumroll)
**Prompt:** Classic orchestral snare drum roll, building tension, sudden stop.

### EFECTE: PREMI (Jackpot)
**Prompt:** Casino slot machine jackpot sound. Coins falling, bells ringing, triumphant brass fanfare.

### EFECTE: ERROR (Buzzer)
**Prompt:** Classic game show "Wrong Answer" buzzer. A low pitch brass "Wah-wah-wah" slide descending.
